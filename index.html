About VEX V5

VEX V5 is an advanced robotics system designed for education and competition. It features smart sensors, a powerful V5 Brain, and user-friendly coding tools such as Python and VEXcode. Our team leverages these tools to design, build, and program innovative robots, preparing for a future in STEM fields.
